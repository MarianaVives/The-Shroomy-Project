The Shroomy Project

Every civilization on Earth has found some explanations to its [fungi] existence, while lending these potential friends or foes its due respect.

For millenia, fungi- or more specifically mushrooms- have been an enigma to humans.
These organisms mysteriously grow out of the ground but unlike plants, they cannot grow their own source of food via the sun.
Unlike animals, they cannot move. And as a matter of fact, just very few people ever thought of mushroom as alive organisms.
However, early pioneers in Botanics observed the nature of these organisms and mistakenly classified fungi within the plant kingdom.
It was until very recently, in 1968, that these peculiar organisms got awarded their own indpendent lineage: "The fungi kingdom".
Please, join this project to help widspread information regarding this resilient kingdom that has so much to offer to humanity.
